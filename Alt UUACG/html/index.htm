WELCOME!

WE ARE SO GLAD YOU ARE HERE!
Unitarian Universalists are known as the Uncommon Denomination.

We welcome everyone, UUCG is a Welcoming Congregation which reaches out a multicultural welcome embracing all cultural aspects, including, for instance, race and ethnicity, class age, sexual orientation, gender identity and expression, ability, and more.

If you are not familiar with us, why not give us a try some Sunday.

OUR SERVICES

We meet at 10:30 am on the first. second and third Sundays of each month, September through June. The service on the second Sunday is put together by our Social Justice Team. There is also a First Sunday Forum at 9:30 on the first Sunday of each month at which Unitarianism is discussed. A forum on Social Justice issues is generally held at the same time on the second Sunday of the month. We have no services in July and August.
